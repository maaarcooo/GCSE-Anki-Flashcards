What are Alpine regions? | Cold, mountainous regions with snowy conditions and warmer summers.
What is Biodiversity? | A large variety of animal and plant life living in the same ecosystem.
What is Climate? | The long term weather conditions of an area.
What is a Cold environment? | Regions of the Earth that experience sustained below freezing temperatures.
What is Environmental fragility? | The concept of an ecosystem being so sensitive to change that even small changes can have large and potentially destructive impacts.
What is Interdependence? | The concept of multiple things depending on each other for survival.
What is Permafrost? | Ground that is permanently frozen for at least two consecutive years.
What are Polar regions? | Areas surrounding the poles (90°N and 90°S).
What is Soil? | The upper layer of the Earth which is composed of organic matter and other materials.
What are Tundra regions? | Areas at high latitudes, characterised by permafrost, low temperatures and short growing seasons.
What is a Wilderness? | An area that is untouched by humans, and therefore remains unaffected by human activity.
